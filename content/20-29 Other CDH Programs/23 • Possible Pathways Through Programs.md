There is no one right way to sequence the workshops, classes, and fellowships offered by the CDH and our campus partners like [Digital Scholarship](https://library.princeton.edu/digital-scholarship) (DiScho) and [Research Computing](https://researchcomputing.princeton.edu/learn/workshops-live-training). But the diagram below suggests some pathways that PhD students might want to follow: from introductory classes, to project-based learning, to advanced training, and finally capstone presentations.
