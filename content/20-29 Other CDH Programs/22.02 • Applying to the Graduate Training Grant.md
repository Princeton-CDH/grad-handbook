Applicants to the Graduate Fellowship must be enrolled Princeton Ph.D. students, from any department and at any stage of their graduate career (including DCE). Please note that visiting students are not eligible. 

For the 2026-27 academic year, Graduate Training Grants will support travel to institutes and workshops **taking place in the summer of 2027. Applications must be submitted by April 1, 2027.**

Payments are administered through SAFE and awards are contingent on the student sending proof of their admittance to the event.

In no more that 750 words, applicants should:

- Provide information about the course or event they plan to attend
- State what they intend to gain from the experience
- Indicate its relevance to their course of study or professional plans
- Specify where they locate their research interests within the humanities
- Demonstrate that they cannot receive similar training at Princeton
- Include details on the data students plan to use at the workshop, if applicable
- Append a budget showing the anticipated costs and indicate other funds received and other sources applied to
