These are the institutes and workshops our students have attended in the past, with support of a [Graduate Training Grant](20-29%20Other%20CDH%20Programs/22%20•%20Graduate%20Training%20Grant.md):

- [Digital Resources and Methods](http://web.sas.upenn.edu/dream-lab/) (DREAM Lab), University of Pennsylvania
- [Digital Humanities Summer Institute](https://dhsi.org/) (DHSI), Montreal
- [Digital Humanities at Oxford Summer School,](https://digital.humanities.ox.ac.uk/digital-humanities-oxford-summer-school) UK
- [European Summer University in Digital Humanities](https://esudh.github.io/), Besançon, France. "A two-week intense university-level program in several key DH areas."
- [ATRIUM Summer School](https://www.dept.aueb.gr/en/unesco/content/atrium-summer-school-2026), Athens, Greece
- [Critical AI Literacies for Resisting and Reclaiming,](https://www.ru.nl/en/education/education-for-professionals/overview/critical-ai-literacies-for-resisting-and-reclaiming) Radboud Universiteit
- [London Rare Books School](https://ies.sas.ac.uk/study-training/summer-schools/london-rare-books-school)
- [Hellenic Studies Summer Institute](https://cdh.princeton.edu/news/2026/01/22/summer-institute-historic-athens-in-3d/) (Athens)
- [IGEL Training School](https://igelsociety.org/events/training-school-2026/), virtual workshop on Empirical Literary Studies
- Northeastern [AI + Data Ethics (AIDE) Summer](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/ethics/aide-summer/), intended for advanced grad students in STEM and philosophy
- EPFL's [Applied Data Science](https://www.epfl.ch/education/continuing-education/applied-data-science-machine-learning/) (online program, self-paced, certificate granting)
