AI --- its research applications, its political economy, and its underlying technology --- are evolving at a breakneck pace. Follow these groups to keep up to date.

# Community- and Justice-Focused

- [AI Now Institute](https://ainowinstitute.org)
- [Algorithmic Justice League](https://www.ajl.org/), Joy Buolamwini
- [Knowing Machines](https://knowingmachines.org/) project, including this [legal explainer on generative AI](https://knowingmachines.org/knowing-legal-machines/legal-explainer)
- [Atlanta Interdisciplinary AI Network](http://aiai.network/), Lauren Klein
- [Distributed AI Research (DAIR) Institute](https://dair-institute.org/), including their [Data Workers' Inquiry](https://data-workers.org/) project.
- [AI for the People](https://aiforthepeopleus.org/about-us/what-we-do/): “To represent the interests of black communities within high level policy meetings within government, industry and led academic research into the development of rights respecting AI.”
- [AI Accountability Lab](https://aial.ie/pages/aiparis/)
- [Centre for the Governance of AI](https://www.governance.ai/)
- [All Tech is Human](https://alltechishuman.org/)
- [Ada Lovelace Institute](https://www.adalovelaceinstitute.org/about/)

## University-Based or Research-Focused

- UC Berkeley [Center for Human-Compatible AI (CHAI)](https://humancompatible.ai/)
- [Berkeley Machine Intelligence Research Institute (MIRI)](https://intelligence.org/), an organization that works on better formal characterizations of the AI safety problem. Founded by Eliezer Yudkowsky.
- Stanford [Center for Research on Foundation Models](https://crfm.stanford.edu/fmti/May-2024/index.html)
- Stanford [Center for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence](https://hai.stanford.edu/) (HAI)
- Stanford’s [One Hundred Year Study on AI](https://ai100.stanford.edu/), releasing reports every 5 years. 2016, 2021 released so far.
- Jokingly: [Center for the Alignment of AI Alignment Centers](https://alignmentalignment.ai/caaac)

# Mapping These Groups & Arguments

- [Mapping AI](https://mapping-ai.org/) focuses on the US AI policy landscape.
- Michel Justen's ["A Guide to the AI Tribes"](https://substack.com/home/post/p-185759007) outlines the schools of thought on AI and its politics.
