## Knowledge Bases

- [DH Now](https://digitalhumanitiesnow.org/), "an experimental, edited publication that highlights and distributes informally published digital humanities scholarship and resources from the open web." Great place for CFPs, job calls, and recent research highlights.
- [Notes in the Computational Humanities](https://github.com/cu-nil/chnotes/tree/master) is a definitive resource for conferences, fellowships, prizes, journals, tutorials, and more, maintained by the Columbia Narrative Intelligence Lab.

## Communities & Conferences

- [Association for Computers and the Humanities](https://ach.org/) (ACH), the US-based professional organization for DH. Offers professional development events and an annual conference (virtual, open registration).
- [Alliance of Digital Humanities Organizations](https://adho.org/) (ADHO), the international umbrella organization that hosts the annual DH conference, each year on a new continent.
- [Digital Humanities Summer Institute](https://dhsi.org/) (DHSI), long-running, one- or two-week summer training program held in Montreal for newcomers and advanced scholars alike.
- [Computational Humanities Research](https://2027.computational-humanities-research.org/) (CHR), annual conference on computational approaches to the humanities. Typically hosted in Europe.

## Social Media

- Bluesky, where most of the academic DH conversation now happens. See especially [Amanda Visconti](https://www.amandavisconti.com/)’s Bluesky [Quickstart Guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1HVx7prLajuTZ_naAC3WyqJu4loaf2FRxPZSHSUxKsgk/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.8zc3zz2j686j), including its "starter packs" of DH scholars to follow. Pam Lach also has a starter pack of Bluesky users for [DH in Libraries](https://go.bsky.app/Fg9dqjc).
- [Digital Humanities Slack](https://digitalhumanities.slack.com/?), "a set of informal, connected chat rooms for the digital humanities and related interests, with over 50 “channels” (chat rooms) devoted to specific topics such as DH teaching, coding, library work, and conferences."
