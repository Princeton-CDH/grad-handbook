The CDH collaborates with a number of Princeton campus units at the intersection of data, computation, and cultural criticism. Graduate students may want to explore the other offerings from:

- PUL [Digital Scholarship](https://library.princeton.edu/services/digital-scholarship), a one-stop-shop introducing researchers to tools for mapping, text mining, data visualization, and digital exhibits.
- [Research Data Service](https://researchdata.princeton.edu/), which provides expert guidance curating and publishing data.
- [Research Computing](https://researchcomputing.princeton.edu/), which maintains the university's cloud computing infrastructure and offers [workshops](https://researchcomputing.princeton.edu/learn/workshops-live-training) on a range of topics.
- [The AI Lab](https://ai.princeton.edu/ai-lab), which supports and expands the scope of AI research at Princeton. See their [Resource Guide](https://ai.princeton.edu/ai-lab/resource-guide?check_logged_in=1) (PU login required).
- PUL [Maps & Geospatial Information Center](https://library.princeton.edu/services/maps-geospatial-information-center) offers specialized guidance for students working with maps.
- [MakeNet](https://makenet.princeton.edu/), a consortium of the university's maker spaces. Resources include 3D scanning and printing, book binding, and more.
- Social scientists should refer to our partners at the [Data Driven Social Science Initiative](https://ddss.princeton.edu/)
- For help creating a website, see PUL's [Project Website Support](https://libguides.princeton.edu/websupport) and the options provided by OIT's [Web Development Services](https://wds.princeton.edu/) (WDS)
- For suggestions and support creating a digital or data-driven classroom assignments, see the McGraw Center's [Digital Assignment Guide](https://mcgrawect.princeton.edu/digital-assignment-guides/)
- For world-class guidance on professional development, please visit [GradFUTURES](https://gradfutures.princeton.edu/), "a dynamic hub that empowers graduate scholars, researchers, and leaders to succeed in graduate school and craft futures that are fully their own." The CDH often partners with GradFUTURES on hosting [University Administrative Fellows](https://gradfutures.princeton.edu/uaf/apply) (UAF's). Please contact us if you are interested in this possibility.
